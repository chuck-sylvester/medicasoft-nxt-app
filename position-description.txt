This is a contract to hire role after 6 months with Medicasoft.

Title: Solution Engineer
Location: Fully Remote
Duration: 6 month temp to hire
Company: MedicaSoft

Job Description:
We are seeking a Solution Engineer to serve as the primary technical partner for a key Health Information Exchange (HIE) customer using our native FHIR data platform.

MedicaSoft Website: https://www.medicasoft.com
MedicaSoft NXT Platform Info: https://www.medicasoft.com/nxt

In this role, you will act as the technical point of contact supporting the customer’s success with our platform, which includes a FHIR repository, FHIR APIs, and an analytics data warehouse. You will lead technical troubleshooting, guide architectural discussions, and help the customer maximize the value of our interoperability and data services.
This is an ideal position for a senior engineer or architect who enjoys working directly with customers on complex healthcare data challenges and bridging the gap between platform engineering and real-world healthcare interoperability needs.
The ideal candidate combines strong architectural thinking with a hands-on approach to troubleshooting, implementation, and operational support. They take ownership of customer outcomes, proactively drive issues to resolution, and are comfortable working directly within APIs, data pipelines, cloud environments, and interoperability workflows to diagnose and solve problems.

Success Profile:
The successful candidate will:
Take ownership of problems and follow them through to completion.
Be comfortable moving between architecture discussions and hands-on technical execution.
Comfortable writing queries, scripts, or lightweight tooling to investigate issues, validate integrations, and improve operational workflows.
Demonstrate persistence, urgency, and accountability in customer-facing situations.
Build trust with both customers and internal teams through reliability and consistent follow-through.
Thrive in environments where initiative, adaptability, and independent problem-solving are critical.

Key Responsibilities:
Strategic Customer Technical Leadership
Serve as the technical lead for a major HIE customer engagement.
Build trusted relationships with customer technical teams, architects, and operational stakeholders.
Act as the primary escalation point for complex technical and data-related issues.
Provide ongoing guidance on best practices for leveraging our FHIR-native platform.
Solution Architecture & Platform Enablement
Lead solution-oriented discussions to help the customer optimize use of:
FHIR repository and resource persistence
FHIR REST APIs and interoperability workflows
Analytics data warehouse integration and reporting
Support architectural planning for new customer initiatives, integrations, and enhancements.
Translate customer needs into actionable technical recommendations and internal follow-up.
Work directly with APIs, FHIR payloads, cloud infrastructure, data pipelines, and platform configurations to validate implementations and resolve operational issues.
Partner with engineering teams on technical improvements, automation opportunities, monitoring enhancements, and operational scalability.
Troubleshooting & Data Issue Resolution
Investigate and resolve complex healthcare data issues involving:
FHIR resource integrity and mapping
Data ingestion and transformation pipelines
Terminology and code set alignment
Downstream analytics and reporting consistency
Collaborate with internal engineering teams to diagnose platform behaviors and deliver timely resolutions.
Develop root cause analyses and corrective action plans for recurring issues.
Perform hands-on investigation and validation within platform environments, APIs, logs, databases, and data pipelines to identify root causes.
Independently drive complex technical issues through resolution rather than relying solely on escalation or delegation.
Take ownership of customer-impacting issues from identification through remediation and communication.
Interoperability & Healthcare Data Expertise
Provide subject matter expertise on healthcare interoperability standards, including:
HL7 FHIR
US Core / USCDI requirements
HL7 v2, CCDA documents, and other legacy feeds (as applicable)
Cross-Functional Collaboration

Hands-on experience with FHIR and healthcare interoperability concepts.
Ability to troubleshoot complex data workflows across operational and analytics environments.
Experience working with cloud-based systems and modern data architectures.
Strong communication skills with the ability to explain technical concepts to customer and internal audiences.
Demonstrated track record of taking ownership of complex technical and customer issues and driving them to successful resolution.
Strong hands-on technical troubleshooting skills across APIs, cloud systems, data pipelines, integrations, and production environments.

Self-directed and proactive with the ability to manage priorities, follow through on commitments, and operate with minimal oversight.
